ETHAN CHO PHOTO

PHOTOGRAPHY PORTFOLIO | 사진 포트폴리오

"I only wish my camera could see the world that I see."


Based in Seoul, South Korea and Duke University, Durham, North Carolina, I’m a photographer who wants to freeze time. My biggest fear is forgetting: forgetting people, moments, landscapes. Photography is not only an avenue for me to remember moments, but share this joy with others. Whether it be events, landscapes, streets, wildlife, or headshots, taking photos lets me make my camera a pseudo time machine—a time machine for business, art, and memories.
